AboutUs

Home construction is one of the biggest challenges that an average person faces in his/her lifetime in Pakistan because of expensive contractors, lack of legitimate information about quality of vendors as well as unreliable contractors. For example if total cost of home construction is 5 million PKR but it will cost 5.5 to 6 million PKR because of contractor’s commission. To minimize the role of contractors and other commission agents we are proposing an idea to make a web based portal which allows customers to book construction related services such as purchasing material, hiring labour and approving building design from architect.

Services

WebCon focuses on minimizing all these risks by providing one stop platform with integrated payment system, and personalized recommendation system. Customers can avail different types of services based on their needs, brand preference, location, service rates and reviews. Customers can search and compare different companies and vendors for the same type of service/material. This improves their decision making process. They can also get discounts on materials during certain time of the year which is helpful in retaining customers. Customers can contact via text/video call with companies on our platform for further details. All of this also helps companies too. Their quality of service directly affects their ratings by getting customer feedback. The better their service is, higher will be the customer retention rate.
